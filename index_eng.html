Reits Global land

Platform trades real estate products in the form of asseted coins

RSGL

Real estate has historically been an immutable asset, and it is still the most popular stable real thing.
However, the domestic real estate investment market has been hit hard, and in line with rapid social changes, blockchain's big data technology and smart contracts are expected to become a new trend in the global real estate market.

Business Model

Buy-in Type

It is a form of actually purchasing real estate with the amount invested in overseas real estate products and distributing monthly rent income and sales income according to the ratio of holding RSGL coins.

A Re-lease Type

It is a form of obtaining a lease sale with the amount invested in a real estate product and allocating monthly rent income from re-lease.

Commercial real estate

Individuals or businesses who want to rent can check information on commercial real estate products on the RSGL platform and pay coins to participate in real estate product transactions.
